Instructions

1- A timer is set for each question. You must answer within the allotted time.
2- Use the "Next" button to move to the next question.
3- You can also use the "Skip" button to skip a question and come back to it later.
4- Only one option must be selected out of the given options for each question.
5- A scoring system is in place:
--- Correct answers earn you +5 points.
--- Incorrect answers result in a deduction of -1 point.
6- The quiz consists of multiple-choice questions.
7- Please answer honestly and to the best of your knowledge.
8- Keep an eye on the timer; time is limited for each question.
9- Your total score will be displayed at the end of the quiz.
